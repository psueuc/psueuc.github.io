About Us

EUC serves as a voice for the students in the College of Engineering, and acts as the bridge between the administration, faculty, and students of the College. Throughout the year, EUC allocates funds for engineering organizations, holds numerous events for the faculty and student body, communicates student ideas to administration and faculty, and acts as part of the Academic Integrity Council.
